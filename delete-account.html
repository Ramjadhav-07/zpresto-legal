Delete Your Zpresto Account

Zpresto users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to zprestoindia@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Zpresto Account
  3. Include your registered email address or phone number

Data that will be deleted

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Account information

Data retention

Some order or legal records may be retained if required by law.

Timeframe

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 working days.
